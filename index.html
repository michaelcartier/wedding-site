Travel & Accommodations

✈️

Flights & Arrival

We recommend flying into Montréal-Trudeau International Airport (YUL). To give yourself time to settle in before the big Saturday celebration, consider arriving on Thursday or Friday. Note: Most international visitors will need an eTA (Electronic Travel Authorization) to enter Canada.

🚕

From the Airport

There is currently no REM train from the airport. Uber is the easiest option (approx. $40 to downtown). Alternatively, the 747 Bus works well and is a cheaper public transit option.

🚇

Getting Around

Montreal is highly walkable, but the best way to get around is the STM Metro system. You can buy a Weekly Pass or a 3-day pass at any metro station kiosk for unlimited rides!

🍂

September Weather

September in Montreal is a beautiful transitional month! The days are often warm and sunny, but the evenings can be quite crisp. We recommend packing light layers for the evening.

🏛️

Downtown & Old Port Vibes

Our venue, Auberge Saint-Gabriel, is located right in the heart of the historic Old Port. Take some time to stroll down the beautiful cobblestone streets, enjoy the riverside views, pop into the amazing local cafes, and take in the European architecture that makes Montreal so special!
