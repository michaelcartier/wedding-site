Our Story

How We Met

It all started quite unexpectedly at work. While we were busy navigating the daily grind, we eventually found ourselves grabbing beers after hours, trading stories and realizing there was a connection that went far beyond the office walls.

As it turns out, the universe had been trying to introduce us for a long time. While we finally met in Montreal, we later discovered a hidden twist in our timelines: years earlier, Karina had lived in the very same town in France where Michael grew up. In a classic "missed connection," he had moved away just as she was arriving to spend a year there.

We may have missed each other across the ocean back then, but we're so glad we finally caught up to one another right here at home.

The Proposal

During a trip to Mexico, Michaël led us on a long walk toward the water. Karina had no idea where they were going, and our son, only two at the time, was happily oblivious to the big moment ahead.

When we reached a spot overlooking the crystal blue sea, Michaël got down on one knee. It was so completely unexpected that Karina's first reaction wasn't shock… it was laughter. For a split second, she honestly thought he might be joking. It was a moment of pure, startled joy that felt perfectly us.

Travel & Accommodations

✈️

Flights & Arrival

We recommend flying into Montréal-Trudeau International Airport (YUL). To give yourself time to settle in before the big Saturday celebration, consider arriving on Thursday or Friday. Note: Most international visitors will need an eTA (Electronic Travel Authorization) to enter Canada.

🚕

From the Airport

There is currently no REM train from the airport. Uber is the easiest option (approx. $40 to downtown). Alternatively, the 747 Bus works well and is a cheaper public transit option.

🚇

Getting Around

Montreal is highly walkable, but the best way to get around is the STM Metro system. You can buy a Weekly Pass or a 3-day pass at any metro station kiosk for unlimited rides!

🍂

September Weather

September in Montreal is a beautiful transitional month! The days are often warm and sunny, but the evenings can be quite crisp. We recommend packing light layers for the evening.

🏛️

Downtown & Old Port Vibes

Our venue, Auberge Saint-Gabriel, is located right in the heart of the historic Old Port. Take some time to stroll down the beautiful cobblestone streets, enjoy the riverside views, pop into the amazing local cafes, and take in the European architecture that makes Montreal so special!
